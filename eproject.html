Food & Nutrition Tips by Veteran Patrona

Golden Rules for Feeding Your Dog

  • Always provide fresh water daily
  • Choose high-quality protein as first ingredient
  • Avoid toxic foods: chocolate, grapes, onions, garlic
  • Puppies need 3-4 meals a day, adults 2
  • Adjust portions according to activity level
  • Never feed cooked bones
